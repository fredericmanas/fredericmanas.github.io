I am an evolutionary biologist with a focus on reproductive physiology and sexual selection. My PhD research examined the mechanisms underlying post-mating sexual selection in the black soldier fly (Hermetia illucens), including sperm degradation in female spermathecae, seminal fluid proteins and their post-mating effects, and the influence of viral infection on sperm storage.

My areas of interest include :
  • Sexual selection
  • Sperm biology
  • Sperm storage organs
  • Sexual conflicts
